HIGH PROTEIN NUTRITION PLAN

A high-protein nutrition plan is perfect for muscle building, recovery, and satiety. It features lean meats, eggs, dairy, legumes, and plant-based proteins. This plan is ideal for athletes and anyone looking to increase their protein intake for strength and fitness.

How it works: Each day includes protein-rich foods at every meal to support muscle repair and growth. Adjust portion sizes and protein sources to fit your dietary preferences and goals.
Day #1
  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach, whole grain toast
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, sweet potato, green beans
  • Dinner: Baked salmon, quinoa, roasted broccoli
  • Snack: Greek yogurt parfait with berries & chia seeds
