PUSH-PULL-LEGS SPLIT

A Push-Pull-Legs (PPL) Split divides workouts into pushing movements, pulling movements, and leg exercises, typically 6 days a week. This approach allows for optimal muscle recovery while maintaining high training frequency.

How it works: You train pushing muscles (chest, shoulders, triceps) one day, pulling muscles (back, biceps) the next, and legs on the third day. This split is excellent for intermediate to advanced lifters looking to maximize muscle growth and strength.
PPL Split #1
  • Day 1: Push
    - Bench Press: 4x8
    - Overhead Press: 4x8
    - Incline Dumbbell Press: 3x10
    - Lateral Raises: 3x12
    - Tricep Pushdowns: 3x12
    - Chest Fly: 3x12
  • Day 2: Pull
    - Deadlift: 4x6
    - Pull-Ups: 4x8
    - Barbell Row: 3x10
    - Face Pulls: 3x12
    - Bicep Curls: 3x12
    - Hammer Curls: 3x12
  • Day 3: Legs
    - Squats: 4x8
    - Romanian Deadlift: 3x10
    - Leg Press: 3x12
    - Leg Curls: 3x12
    - Calf Raises: 4x15
    - Walking Lunges: 3x12
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Push (repeat)
  • Day 6: Pull (repeat)
  • Day 7: Legs (repeat)
PPL Split #2
  • Day 1: Push
    - Incline Bench Press: 4x8
    - Arnold Press: 4x10
    - Dips: 3x10
    - Front Raises: 3x12
    - Skullcrushers: 3x12
    - Cable Fly: 3x12
  • Day 2: Pull
    - Barbell Row: 4x8
    - Lat Pulldown: 4x10
    - T-Bar Row: 3x10
    - Rear Delt Fly: 3x12
    - Preacher Curls: 3x12
    - Concentration Curls: 3x12
  • Day 3: Legs
    - Front Squat: 4x8
    - Bulgarian Split Squat: 3x10
    - Hip Thrust: 3x12
    - Leg Extension: 3x12
    - Seated Calf Raise: 4x15
    - Step-Ups: 3x12
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Push (repeat)
  • Day 6: Pull (repeat)
  • Day 7: Legs (repeat)
PPL Split #3
  • Day 1: Push
    - Dumbbell Press: 4x10
    - Military Press: 4x8
    - Incline Fly: 3x12
    - Upright Row: 3x12
    - Tricep Extension: 3x12
    - Push-Ups: 3x15
  • Day 2: Pull
    - Chin-Ups: 4x8
    - One-Arm Dumbbell Row: 4x10
    - Cable Row: 3x12
    - Shrugs: 3x12
    - Reverse Curls: 3x12
    - Cable Curls: 3x12
  • Day 3: Legs
    - Sumo Deadlift: 4x6
    - Goblet Squat: 4x10
    - Leg Curl: 3x12
    - Calf Press: 4x15
    - Box Jumps: 3x10
    - Farmer's Walk: 3x30s
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Push (repeat)
  • Day 6: Pull (repeat)
  • Day 7: Legs (repeat)
