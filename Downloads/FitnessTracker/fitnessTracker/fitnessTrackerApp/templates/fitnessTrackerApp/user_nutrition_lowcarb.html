LOW CARB NUTRITION PLAN

A low-carb nutrition plan focuses on healthy fats, lean proteins, and plenty of non-starchy vegetables. This plan is great for weight management, blood sugar control, and those looking to reduce carbohydrate intake.

How it works: Each day features meals that are low in carbs but rich in nutrients and flavor. Adjust portion sizes and ingredients to fit your needs and preferences.
Day #1
  • Breakfast: Egg omelette with spinach & feta
  • Lunch: Grilled salmon, avocado salad
  • Dinner: Chicken stir-fry with bell peppers & broccoli
  • Snack: Celery sticks with almond butter
Day #2
  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with walnuts & raspberries
  • Lunch: Beef lettuce wraps with salsa
  • Dinner: Zucchini noodles with pesto & cherry tomatoes
  • Snack: Cheese sticks & cucumber slices
Day #3
  • Breakfast: Smoothie (unsweetened almond milk, spinach, protein powder, peanut butter)
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken Caesar salad (no croutons)
  • Dinner: Baked cod, roasted Brussels sprouts, cauliflower mash
  • Snack: Hard-boiled eggs
