FULL BODY SPLIT

A Full Body Split works all major muscle groups in each session, typically 3 days a week. This approach is great for beginners and those with limited time, as it provides frequent stimulation to all muscle groups.

How it works: You train your entire body in each workout session, focusing on compound movements that work multiple muscle groups simultaneously. This split allows for more recovery time between sessions and is excellent for building overall strength and muscle mass.
Full Body Split #1
  • Day 1: Full Body
    - Squats: 4x8
    - Bench Press: 4x8
    - Deadlift: 3x6
    - Overhead Press: 3x10
    - Pull-Ups: 3x8
    - Plank: 3x60s
  • Day 2: Rest
  • Day 3: Full Body
    - Deadlift: 4x6
    - Incline Bench Press: 4x8
    - Bulgarian Split Squat: 3x10
    - Lat Pulldown: 3x10
    - Shoulder Press: 3x10
    - Russian Twists: 3x15
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Full Body (repeat Day 1)
  • Day 6: Rest
  • Day 7: Rest or Active Recovery
