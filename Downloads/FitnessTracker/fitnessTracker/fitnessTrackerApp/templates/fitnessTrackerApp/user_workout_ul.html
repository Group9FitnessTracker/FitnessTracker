UPPER/LOWER SPLIT

An Upper/Lower Split alternates between upper body and lower body workouts, typically 4 days a week. This approach allows for optimal recovery and muscle growth by focusing on major muscle groups each session.

How it works: You train your upper body one day, lower body the next, rest, and repeat. This is a great way for beginners to get stronger, learn proper form, and recover well. Each workout targets all the main muscles for that day, and you can adjust the exercises and weights as you progress.
Upper/Lower Split #1
  • Day 1: Upper Body
    - Bench Press: 4x8
    - Pull-Ups: 3x8
    - Shoulder Press: 3x10
    - Barbell Row: 3x10
    - Tricep Dips: 3x12
    - Bicep Curls: 3x12
  • Day 2: Lower Body
    - Squats: 4x8
    - Romanian Deadlift: 3x10
    - Leg Press: 3x12
    - Calf Raises: 3x15
    - Walking Lunges: 3x12 (each leg)
  • Day 3: Rest
  • Day 4: Upper Body (repeat)
  • Day 5: Lower Body (repeat)
  • Day 6: Rest
  • Day 7: Rest or Active Recovery
